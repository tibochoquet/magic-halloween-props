# DESIGN_RULES.md

## Brand Identity

* cinematic
* dark
* immersive
* premium
* halloween-focused
* atmospheric
* mysterious
* realistic
* high-quality
* modern horror

## Visual Style

* dark charcoal/black backgrounds
* orange ember glow accents
* subtle fog overlays
* cinematic lighting
* soft gradients
* realistic shadows
* premium spacing
* minimal clutter

## Typography

* cinematic headings
* premium typography hierarchy
* large readable titles
* generous spacing
* modern ecommerce feel

Avoid:

* cartoon horror
* childish halloween styling
* cluttered layouts
* oversaturated orange
